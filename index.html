Hey there!

My name is Ashli Hanson and I am a Full Stack web developer residing in Austin, Texas!

I've spent the majority of my professional career in the service industry and customer service. Most recently I was a shop manager for Summer Moon Coffee for 3 years at the Buda and Trailer locations. Being a manager in a fast paced environment, like a coffee shop, really sharpened my problem solving skills and multitasking abilities.

I have chosen a career in the web developing world because I love all that is has to offer. Using my brain and being challenged are very important to me but I also love having the option to be creative and design.

Another big draw to this career is the impact developers can really have on the world. The ability to share information to anyone and everywhere is a gift that I'm excited to work on and take seriously. I want to use these skills to share important information that should be easily accessible to anyone.

Eventually, I would love to work fully remote with an organization I fully support so I can work while I travel to the national and state parks across the country.
